Role: Fabrication team, Design Team, Testing team
Date: Fall 2015



Petra is a ascending and repelling device for climbers, cavers,
and canyoneers with an additional life saving feature. This
purely mechanical device is able to automatically catch a person
if they reach the end of their rope whilst decending, by a cam
lock system. Created, designed, and fabricated in a team of 22,
Petra was the product of 2.009, Product Engineering Processes,
the capstone class for Mechanical Engineering at MIT.

We were able to develop a fully functional alpha prototype. We tested Petra using weights outdoors, as well as using an instron machine indoors. We were able to meet our goal of being able to comply with the current rules and regulations for climbing devices for use in all different weather conditions.

This device was fabricated by use of the following machines: mill, lathe, waterjet, laser cutter.
