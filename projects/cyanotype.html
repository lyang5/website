Project: Transcultural Suit
Date: Fall 2015


As the product of the following prompt, "design wearable art to foster an understanding between cultures....embracing elements of your own identity to make connections with other cultures."

I decided to use a photographic printing process to imprint designs on sqaures of fabric which I then sewed together to make a quilt. The templates were designed in Adobe Illustrator and printed out on transparencies. Half of the squares are personal, while the others are of a friends who's cultural background is different than my own.
