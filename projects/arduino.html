Role: Mechanical Engineer Intern
Date: Summer 2016



In order to test the durability of massage modules (airbags), I designed a circuit with two pressure sensors (for safety in case of a malfunction), a pump, and valves to monitor the pressure inside the airbags to ensure there was no leakage happening at a pressure slightly above the maximum pressure the product reaches. The system was run continuously for different airbags until either there was a dip in pressure, indicating leakage, or until the number of cycles it had completed was well above our life span calculations.
